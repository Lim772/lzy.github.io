Our Facilities

Our university offers top facilities, including the library, sports stadium, advanced chemistry labs,
modern ICT labs, and more supporting both academic excellence and student life.

Library

The university library offers extensive resources, quiet study spaces, and collaborative areas supporting research and academic excellence.

Sport Stadium

The university boasts the largest sports stadium on campus, providing top-tier facilities for athletics, training, and major sporting events.

Laboratory

Our university laboratories, including advanced chemistry and BIO labs, supporting practical learning, research, and innovation.

ICT Lab

Our university's computer lab is fully equipped with high-performance iMac desktops, offering students access to the latest creative and professional software.

Music Room

At The University of Singapore, learning meets creativity in this bright, tiered classroom. From inspiring lectures to piano-filled rehearsals, students can even enjoy creating their own band here.

Swimming Pool

Our campus pool at The University of Singapore is the perfect spot to relax, train, or just float your stress away after class.
