Easy Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 small onion, garlic and ginger, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons curry poweder
  • 1 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • half cup coconut milk

Steps:

    Step 1 - Ingredient preparation
  1. Seal the chicken in a pan with a little oil.
  2. Add in chopped onion, garlic and ginger.
    1. Cook for 5 minutes to soften.
    2. Add in curry powder and cinnamon
    • Stir and cook for a further minute.
  3. Add in tomato paste and coconut milk.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes - until the chicken is cooked through.
